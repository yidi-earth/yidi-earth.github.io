Why Convivial?

(This is part of the series L’Only Child Syndrome)

An initiative

I’m living in the void of time and space. I have arrived at a still point in life. As if I’m standing in front of this tranquil and magnificent palace. The exquisite details and lifelike reliefs marks its significance as a piece of artwork. A comprehension of mortality, hence immortality. I have always suspected my whole life that I am destined for this place. I do not expect to have all the answers as humility goes a long way. Yet that hightened awareness of perception, appreciation, and invincibility, resulted in an unfalsifiable optimism, which seems to be the rarest commodity. Discovering such a treasure calls for a celebration for the tiny victory.
In a way, it’s a rest stop. I am translating all the discoveries to the uninitiated minds.

I would like to backtrace the winding and forking paths just in case anyone is interested to use as a reference, or simply to get some rapport at the bottom of the valley.
