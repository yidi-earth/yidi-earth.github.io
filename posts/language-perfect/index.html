## The Question

Why do you like the things you do? Think about it. Do you actually have any free will over your choice?

Why do you prefer Pepsi over Coca Cola? What if this was a world without Cola at all?

For a while I wanted to rewire the reward system in my brain by lying to myself. It wasn’t a very smart idea. I was very proud of being a brain hacker though. Becoming self-conscious of my own decision making process is the first step to shift perspectives.

Why is it important to shift perspectives? The short answer is, if you don’t do it proactively, someone else is then doing it to you. Just look at all the billboards all over the street. The simple rule is that the more exposure something gets, the more people are attracted to it. Well then you might ask, isn’t it too easy? Too obvious? It is indeed so.

This is an attempt to comb through the inner journey that I’ve been through to arrive at where I am today. The title screamed its way out. I couldn’t stop the information flow.
